Hello!

This blog was created with the purpose in mind to provide readers quick overviews of animation. How it works:

  1. We select a show and watch every episode available.
  2. We give a description plus our honest review and open a discussion into what experience the show presented to us
  3. We provide NO SPOILERS unless stated in the title
  4. We post every Thursday and Friday!

Though the main focus is anime, we also love other forms of animation so be ready for other content to be mixed in from time to time!

P.S. We love participation and want to hear your opinions too!
